O5 Ranks/What They Do

The O5 Council is the highest decision-making body within the SCP Foundation. Their primary responsibilities include overseeing the Foundation's operations, making critical decisions regarding containment protocols, and ensuring the secrecy of the Foundation's activities. They operate in secrecy and are typically the only individuals with access to the most classified information.
