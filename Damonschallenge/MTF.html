What are MTFs?

Mobile Task Forces (MTFs) are specialized units of the SCP Foundation, designed to respond rapidly to various anomalous situations. Each MTF is composed of personnel with unique skills and expertise, enabling them to handle a wide range of threats effectively.

Purpose of MTFs

MTFs serve multiple crucial functions within the SCP Foundation, including:

  • Containment: Securing and containing anomalous entities that pose a threat to public safety.
  • Investigation: Conducting field investigations to gather information on anomalous events and locations.
  • Recovery: Retrieving SCP objects and personnel from hazardous situations.
  • Neutralization: Eliminating threats that cannot be contained through other means.
  • Support: Providing assistance to other Foundation personnel during operations.

Operations

MTFs are deployed in various operational contexts, including:

  • Response to containment breaches.
  • Investigation of new anomalies reported by field agents.
  • Collaboration with other MTFs and Foundation departments.
  • Conducting research on newly discovered anomalies.

Conclusion

Mobile Task Forces play a vital role in the SCP Foundation's mission to protect humanity from the unknown. Their specialized training and readiness enable them to confront threats head-on, ensuring the safety and secrecy of the Foundation's operations.
