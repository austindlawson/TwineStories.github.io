About Us

The Rapid Response Team is an elite unit within the SCP Foundation, tasked with addressing high-risk incidents involving anomalous entities. Comprised of specialists from various fields, our team ensures that containment protocols are upheld and that the public remains safe from SCP threats.

Mission

Our mission is to provide immediate and effective responses to anomalous events. We are committed to the preservation of life and the containment of threats posed by SCP entities. Our team operates under the strictest confidentiality and adherence to Foundation protocols.

What We Do

  • Conduct immediate assessments of SCP-related incidents.
  • Coordinate with containment teams and local authorities during crises.
  • Provide medical and tactical support during SCP containment operations.
  • Implement emergency protocols to secure public safety.
  • Perform drills and training to maintain readiness for various scenarios.
  • Analyze and document incidents for future response improvements.

Services

  • Emergency Anomaly Containment
  • Threat Assessment and Mitigation
  • Medical Response for SCP-related Injuries
  • Collaborative Operations with Local Authorities
  • Training Programs for Foundation Personnel
