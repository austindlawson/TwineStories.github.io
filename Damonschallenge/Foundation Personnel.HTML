Introduction

The SCP Foundation Personnel are dedicated to managing and containing anomalous entities while ensuring the safety of all personnel involved. Each member plays a critical role in the Foundation's operations, working together to handle the unpredictable nature of anomalies.

Roles and Responsibilities

The primary roles of SCP Foundation Personnel include:

  • Research Personnel:
    • Conduct experiments and gather data on anomalous entities.
    • Document findings and update containment procedures as necessary.
  • Security Personnel:
    • Maintain site security and ensure the safe containment of anomalies.
    • Respond to breaches and emergencies promptly.
  • Medical Personnel:
    • Provide medical assistance to personnel exposed to anomalous effects.
    • Conduct health assessments and psychological evaluations.
  • Administrative Staff:
    • Manage documentation and personnel records.
    • Coordinate communication between departments.
  • Field Agents:
    • Investigate anomalous occurrences and report findings.
    • Engage with local authorities as necessary.
