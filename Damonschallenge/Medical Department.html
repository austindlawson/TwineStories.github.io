Welcome to the official page of the SCP Medical Department. Our mission is to ensure the health and well-being of all personnel within the Foundation while managing the unique medical needs associated with anomalous entities and incidents.

Department Functions:

  • Medical Treatment:
    • Administer medical care for all Foundation personnel.
    • Provide emergency medical response for incidents involving anomalies.
    • Conduct regular health assessments and screenings.
  • Psycho-medical Support:
    • Offer psychological evaluations and counseling services.
    • Develop support programs for staff dealing with anomalous exposure.
    • Conduct trauma debriefings after critical incidents.
  • Research on Anomalies:
    • Study the effects of anomalous entities on human physiology and psychology.
    • Collaborate with other departments to enhance understanding of SCP-related health risks.
  • Training and Education:
    • Provide training for personnel on health and safety protocols.
    • Conduct workshops on recognizing symptoms of anomalous exposure.
    • Regularly update staff on best practices for medical emergencies.
  • Containment Medical Procedures:
    • Implement specific medical protocols for handling personnel involved with SCPs.
    • Assist in the evaluation and treatment of personnel affected by containment breaches.
  • Collaboration with Other Departments:
    • Work closely with the Security and Containment Departments to ensure the safety of medical operations.
    • Participate in multidisciplinary teams for incident response and recovery.

Protocols and Procedures:

The Medical Department follows strict protocols to maintain health and safety:

  • Mandatory health checks for personnel exposed to anomalous entities.
  • Immediate reporting of any medical incidents or health concerns.
  • Implementation of strict hygiene and containment measures during medical procedures.

Note:

All personnel are reminded to prioritize their health and well-being. Regular check-ups and adherence to medical protocols are essential to ensuring a safe work environment.
