Welcome to the official page of the SCP Scientific Department. Our primary objective is to understand and contain anomalous entities, ensuring public safety while exploring the unknown.

Researcher Ranks:

  • Trainee Researcher - Rank 1
  • Junior Researcher - Rank 2
  • Researcher - Rank 3
  • Senior Researcher - Rank 4
  • Head Researcher - Rank 5
  • Assistant Board Director - Rank 6
  • Board Director - Rank 7
  • O5 Council - In charge (O5-#)

Department Functions:

  • Personnel Security:
    • Conduct thorough background checks and maintain personnel security clearances.
    • Implement security screenings for all new hires and visitors.
    • Regularly review and update security clearance levels based on role changes.
  • Site Security:
    • Monitor and secure all Foundation sites to prevent breaches and unauthorized access.
    • Install and maintain surveillance systems and physical barriers.
    • Conduct regular security audits and risk assessments of all facilities.
  • Containment Operations:
    • Assist in the containment of anomalous entities according to established protocols.
    • Collaborate with the Containment Division to develop and improve containment strategies.
    • Perform routine inspections and maintenance of containment measures.
  • Emergency Response:
    • Coordinate responses to security incidents and containment breaches.
    • Develop and maintain emergency response plans for various scenarios.
    • Train personnel in emergency protocols and conduct drills regularly.
  • Training and Drills:
    • Provide regular training sessions for security personnel on protocols and response strategies.
    • Conduct drills to simulate various security scenarios and evaluate readiness.
    • Offer specialized training in advanced security techniques and anomaly handling.
  • Intelligence Gathering:
    • Collect and analyze data on potential security threats to the Foundation.
    • Collaborate with other departments to identify and assess anomalies that may pose risks.
  • Collaboration with Law Enforcement:
    • Establish and maintain relationships with local and national law enforcement agencies.
    • Coordinate joint operations for incidents that exceed Foundation capabilities.
