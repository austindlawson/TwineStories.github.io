Welcome

Secure. Contain. Protect.

Mission Statement: To safeguard humanity from anomalous entities and phenomena that pose a threat to normalcy.

This agency operates under strict confidentiality to prevent public panic and maintain societal order.

SCP Database

Search and explore various SCP entities that have been classified based on their threat levels:

  • Safe: Entities that can be easily contained.
  • Euclid: Entities that require more complex containment procedures.
  • Keter: Entities that pose a significant threat and are difficult to contain.

Access to detailed SCP files is restricted to authorized personnel only.

Research & Development

Our research teams are dedicated to studying anomalies to better understand their properties and potential risks.

Current projects include the investigation of anomalous objects, reality-bending phenomena, and interdimensional entities.

Field Operations

Field agents are deployed to secure and contain anomalies, often in dangerous situations.

Operations are conducted with utmost caution to minimize exposure to the public and potential breaches of security.
