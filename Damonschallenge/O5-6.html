Personnel Designation: O5-6

Object Class: Redacted

Special Containment Procedures:

O5-6 does not require any physical containment. The identity and actions of O5-6 are known only to a select group of Level 5 personnel. All communications and directives issued by O5-6 are to be logged and monitored for compliance with Foundation protocol.

Description:

O5-6 is the designation for the sixth member of the O5 Council, the apex governing body of the SCP Foundation. This individual is recognized for their expertise in research and development of anomalous technologies.

O5-6 serves as the head of the Foundation's Research and Development Division, overseeing the creation and application of technology related to anomalous entities.

Responsibilities:

O5-6 oversees the design and testing of new containment technologies, coordinates research projects, and ensures that innovative solutions are integrated into the Foundation's operations.

Abilities:

Innovative Thinker: O5-6 has a remarkable ability to devise creative solutions to complex problems involving anomalous technologies.

Technical Expertise: O5-6 possesses extensive knowledge of various scientific fields, enabling them to lead multidisciplinary teams effectively.

Background:

O5-6’s history is largely classified. They have been instrumental in the development of several key technologies that enhance the Foundation’s ability to contain and study anomalies.

Addendum O5-6-A:

O5-6 played a pivotal role in the development of the latest containment procedures for several high-risk entities, significantly improving safety protocols.

Addendum O5-6-B:

Recent evaluations of O5-6’s projects have led to the establishment of new guidelines for the ethical use of anomalous technologies.

Incident Log O5-6-01:

During a Council meeting, O5-6 proposed a collaborative project with outside organizations to further explore the potential of certain anomalous technologies, igniting a debate on the risks and rewards of such partnerships.

Note:

Personnel are advised to treat communications from O5-6 with the utmost seriousness. The ramifications of O5-6’s directives can be significant, and adherence to their instructions is essential for the continued operation of the Foundation.
